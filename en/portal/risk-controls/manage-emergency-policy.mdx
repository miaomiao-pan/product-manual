Emergency policy is designed for urgent risk situations and can quickly strengthen the safety of your transactions. When risks are detected, create an emergency policy to immediately enforce auto rejection or approval quorum requirements across your organization. While this provides immediate protection, be sure to investigate and address underlying risks.

<Info>The emergency policy applies to all transactions initiated from Cobo Portal, except for transfers between Sub Accounts and associated Main Accounts of Exchange Wallets.</Info>

<Note>Prerequisite: By default, permission to manage the emergency policy is restricted to Admins.</Note>

## Create an emergency policy

1. Log into [Cobo Portal](https://portal.cobo.com/login).

2. Click <img src="/en/images/common/wallets_icon.svg" className="icon"/> and then click **Transaction Policies**.

3. Click **Manage Emergency Policy**.

4. In the pop-up window, select one of the following actions:
   - **Auto Rejection**: Any transaction will be automatically rejected.  
     <Note>This policy will take effect immediately and does not require approval.</Note>
   - **Approval Quorum**: Any transaction will require a specified number of approvals to proceed.  
     <Note>As this policy may affect the safety of existing transaction policies, it will take effect only after it passes the approval workflow defined by the governancy policy “Manage Transaction Policy.”</Note>

   <img src="/en/images/risk-controls/emergency-policy-create.png" className="screenshot_full_screen" alt="create emergency policy" />

5. Click **Submit** to implement the policy.

6. Complete MFA verification when prompted.

7. Once created, the emergency policy will appear at the top of the **Transaction Policies** list with the highest priority.

## Edit an emergency policy

You can edit an existing emergency policy in one of the following ways on the **Transaction Policies** page:
- Click the **Manage Emergency Policy** button
- Locate your emergency policy at the top of the list, click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_more.svg" className="icon" alt="overflow icon showing more options"/> under **Operations**, and click **Edit**

In the pop-up window, make your changes and submit:
- Switch from Auto Rejection to Approval Quorum (requires approval workflow based on the governancy policy “Manage Transaction Policy”)
- Switch from Approval Quorum to Auto Rejection (takes effect immediately)
- Modify Approval Quorum settings (requires approval workflow)

## Delete an emergency policy

1. On the **Transaction Policies** page, locate your emergency policy at the top of the list.
2. Click <img src="/en/images/common/icon_more.svg" className="icon" alt="overflow icon showing more options"/> under **Operations** and click **Delete**.
3. The deletion requires completing the relevant approval workflow based on your governancy policy.
